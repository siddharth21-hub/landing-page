AI & Knowledge

Custom Knowledge Base

You have a pile of documents: reports, SOPs, old emails, product catalogues. I organise all of it into a system where you can ask a question in plain language and get the right answer instantly. Like a well-read assistant who has read everything you have ever written.

Research & Documents

Large Document Summarisation

You have a 200-page report, a legal contract, or a research paper that needs to be understood quickly. I extract what actually matters for your specific purpose and deliver a clear summary with the key points and recommended actions. No reading the whole thing required.

Multi-source Research

Information is spread across ten different documents or sources. Some agree, some contradict, some are outdated. I analyse all of them together and surface the patterns, the gaps, and the conclusions that are not obvious from reading any one of them individually.

Technical Simplification

You have content written for experts (technical documentation, legal language, academic writing, compliance material) that regular people cannot make sense of. I rewrite it in plain language without losing the accuracy. The idea stays the same. The words become readable.

Automation & Scripting

Workflow Automation

Every business has tasks that repeat on a schedule: reports sent, data moved from one place to another, summaries generated the same way every week. I find those tasks and build a system that handles them automatically. You stop doing the repetitive parts.

Spreadsheet Automation

You have an Excel or Google Sheet that someone spends hours updating manually every week. I set it up so calculations run, reports generate, and data sorts itself, without anyone touching it.

WhatsApp Business Automation

When a customer messages your WhatsApp Business number at 2am asking about your product, they get an instant, accurate reply without you waking up. Catalogue sent, follow-ups scheduled, common questions answered. You only step in for conversations that actually need a human.

Data Scraping

There is publicly available data on the internet that would be useful to you: competitor pricing, market listings, contact information from directories. Instead of manually copying it one entry at a time, I build a tool that pulls it automatically and delivers it as a clean, organised spreadsheet.

Web Applications

Calculators & Quote Generators

Booking & Appointment Systems

Internal tools built specifically for how your business runs: track stock, generate invoices automatically, send them to the right people. Replaces the combination of WhatsApp, Excel, and memory that most small businesses currently run on.

Data Dashboards
